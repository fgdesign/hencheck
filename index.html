Who Am I?

I have interacted with beings from other dimensions since I was a child. I had no idea what or who they were, and yes many times I was frightened. From my child 's bed they would assemble in my bedroom , all sorts. Most of the time it was me looking at them, and they looking at me. There was a language issue, but in my experience I felt things from them, like a telepathy, or it was telepathic, not sure. Some later would be identified as angels or ET's. The visits were many times only others Angelic. Them coming all at the same time turned into separate visits where the teaching would happen. I did not retrieve the information until many years later when I stepped onto the path of doing Spiritual Work.

There was no one in my world when I was a child that could help me with it all, so I buried it. But later it emerged as what I call now THE KNOWING. It's a gut understanding of what someone is going through.  I receive information on what to tell them to help them heal. To end the suffering so they can proceed in life with knowledge of what the (negative) can often times teach us. To enable them to become who they were meant to be, like myself.

These experiences molded who I was and who I was to become. The Intuitive, the Psychic, The Mystic, The Channel ...The Healer which is who I am today.

Thank you for your presence here at this moment in time. If anything here has resonated with you then make an appointment. There may be something you need to know, or maybe not now but later. I for one will look forward to our time together. It's why I am here on this planet...to heal, through caring and love. Until we connect, take care of yourself with kindness towards yourself first, then others. We cannot lead where we have not been

What you can Expect in our Session?

The session will begin with you revealing your concerns and asking me questions. As we speak together, I will be aligning with your energy to begin the healing work. At the same time, I will connect with my Spirit Guides and Teachers as well as yours.

I will share their messages with you so that you can gain valuable insight on how to resolve your concerns, and insight into others to better understand the larger picture of where you are now. I also want to tell you that sometimes loved ones who have passed on can make their presence known.

This is to communicate a message to you usually with great love. If you are comfortable , then I will allow the connection to be made. However, if not then it will not go forward. This decision I leave to you.

The goal of each session is to leave feeling energetically lighter, having gained the wisdom of how to move forward in your life with clarity. It is my sincerest hope that you will feel better after our time together.
